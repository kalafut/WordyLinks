wordy links

What?

Quickly associate a URL with a set of keywords that you choose and later jump to that address using those keywords. Keywords can be anything you'll find memorable. Keyword order matters, but spaces and capitalization don't.

Why?

Sometimes you just need to get an unwieldy URL between devices. Shortened URLs aren't memorable, and other options usually involve bookmarks, sending yourself emails, etc. Wordy Links requires no accounts, apps or syncing.

Are keyword entries deleted?

No, but after 30 days of disuse, someone else can associate them with a different URL.
